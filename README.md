#**Interstellar(2015)**
A captivating science fiction film directed by Ridley Scott.

##**Premise**
During a crewed mission to Mars, astronaut Mark Watney (Matt Damon) is presumed dead after a fierce storm and left behind by his team. Against all odds, Watney survives and must use his ingenuity and scientific knowledge to find a way to signal Earth that he is alive.

##**Key Highlights**
-Emphasis on science and engineering
-Survival against the odds
-Global collaboration
-Stunning Martian landscapes

Favorite quote from the movie ==

###*Behind the Scenes*
The film is based on the acclaimed novel by Andy Weir, who meticulously researched the scientific plausibility of Watney's survival techniques.
